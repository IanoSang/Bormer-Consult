About Us

Bormer Consult seeks to continue compliance, transparency and assignee satisfaction across the corporate immigration in Kenya & Africa .

Bormer Consult is a Kenyan privately owned company founded in 2018 and headquartered in Nairobi.

We assist corporate clients and expatriates with operations and installations in Kenya. We offer customized business immigration services and relocation services to ensure that our clients have convenient transitions.

OUR SERVICES

We consult and guide on the requirements and procedures of applying for Kenya immigration residence status including:-

  • Work Permit
  • Special Pass
  • Dependent Pass
  • Re-Entry
  • Internship Pass
  • Citizenship
  • Permanent Residence
  • Student/Pupil Pass
  • Alien Registration
  • Visa extension
  • KRA PIN
  • NHIF
  • NSSF
  • Office and home space
  • Multiple/Single Journey Visa
We also assist in preparation and submission of applications the ministry of immigration department, monitor and update on their progress.
We establish a good rapport with our clients and the department of immigration to ensure seamless operations and high standard service delivery.
